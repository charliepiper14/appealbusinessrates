Why Rates Are Wrong

Common Reasons Properties Are Overvalued

These issues affect thousands of UK businesses, and most don't even know it

Incorrect Floor Area

VOA measurements may include areas that don't exist or have been changed.

Mezzanine Still Included

Removed mezzanines often remain on valuation records.

Wrong Property Class

Office rates applied to warehouse space, or retail rates on trade counters.

Split Units Not Updated

Subdivided or amalgamated units with outdated valuations.

Poor Condition Not Reflected

Building defects, limited access, or environmental factors not considered.

Comparables Paying Less

Similar properties in your area with lower rates per square metre.
